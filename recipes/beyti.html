Ingredients

  • 2 phyllo dough

For Meat

  • 400 gr ground beef
  • one egg
  • one clove of garlic
  • one onion
  • 100 gr breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp chili pepper

For Sauce

  • 200 gr tomato paste
  • one tsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Method

  1. Mix onion, egg and garlic in blender.

  2. Add the ground beef, breadcrumbs, black pepper, paprika, salt and the mixture in the blender to the mixing bowl.

  3. Knead the mixture .

  4. Lay baking paper on a baking tray and arrange the meatballs in a long and flat shape on the tray. Make sure the meatballs are not thick. Bake the meatballs in a preheated oven at 190 degrees until they are golden brown.

  5. When the meatballs are cooked, roll out half the dough on the counter and spread melted butter on it.

  6. Arrange the cooked meatballs on the phyllo so that there are no spaces.

  7. Slice the roll obliquely.
  8. Spread butter on the phyllo and put it back in the oven. Meanwhile, start preparing the Beytini sauce. Melt the butter in a pan and add the tomato zest and boil for a few minutes.
  9. After adding black pepper and salt on it, take it off the stove.
  10. Take the fried beyti out of the oven, pour the sauce on them and serve with yoghurt. Enjoy your meal!
