Ingredients

  • 600 gr mince
  • one tsp chili pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • one tsp butter
  • one capia pepper

Method

  1. Add chili pepper to mince and knead for 15 min.

  2. After 15 min, add chopped capia pepper and butter, keep knead for 5 more min.

  3. Your well knead mince should look like this.If it is, that mean we are ready for put mince to sish.

  4. Take pieces from the minced meat and put them on the sish.

  5. Let them rest in refrigerator for 5 hour.

  6. Heat the oven to 180C. After 20 min your kebab will be ready

  7. You can serve with garniture and ayran.Bon appetit!
