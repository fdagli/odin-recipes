Ingredients

  • 50 gr butter
  • one clove of garlic
  • 250 gr cubed meat
  • 1 tsp pepper paste
  • 1/4 tps ground red pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the eggplant mix

  • 250 gr yogurt
  • 3 roasted eggplant
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Method

  1. Take the butter in a deep pan. Add the garlic and saut√© lightly.

  2. Then add finely chopped cubed meats and mix, close the lid and leave on low heat for 6-7 minutes until the water is released and cooked.

  3. After the meat is cooked, add tomato paste, ground red pepper and salt to taste and remove.

  4. Chop the roasted eggplants on the cutting board with a sharp knife.

  5. Chop until look like this and add pinch of salt.

  6. When they are well mashed, combine them with the yogurt in a bowl and mixed well.

  7. Take the yoghurt and eggplant mixture into the serving plate. Hole in the middle. Place the prepared meat on it. Serve with the sauce..Bon appetit!
